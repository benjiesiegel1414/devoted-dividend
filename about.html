Why I Invest in Dividends

Dividend investing isn't just about chasing high yields. It's about building a growing stream of cash flow that compounds over time. My goal is financial freedom: generating enough passive income to cover expenses without having to sell shares.

I focus on dividend growth stocks, high-quality ETFs (like SCHD and others), and monthly/weekly payers that provide steady income. I believe in patience, diversification, and reinvesting dividends aggressively in the early years.
